ME BEFORE YOU

Book Cover Illustration

Self-directed Project

I created a narrative illustration for one of my favourite book – Me Before You. The book cover and the dust jacket will be created by you with a specific client in mind. The before step was the industry research I’ve done finding publishing houses that probably hire within my chosen style.

Me Before You is a romance novel written by Jojo Moyes. The book was first published on 5 January 2012 in the United Kingdom. I illustrated a woman face that looks happy on one side. And on the other side, the mask has 2 meanings, 1 represents her unhappy past before she met the man of her life, the second is her painful future when that man was gone.
