MES AMIES

Clothing Brand

Self-directed Project

Creat an entire clothing brand on my own. This requires a lot of researches, from the target customers to competitors, from the story, the vision to the whole branding process.

With the name MES AMIES, it means 'my friends', I hope that every item customers buy will become a friend of them, always be with them. Additionally, the word ‘friends’ also relates to the tone of communication I want to use with the customers. I want the brand to be as friendly as possible, as thoughtful as possible with the customers.
