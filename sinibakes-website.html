SINI BAKES Website

Self-directed Project

One of the projects of my Web Design class, the subject was picking a local business and design a website for them. I visited this local bakery quite often and I’m obsessed with their cupcakes. So I decided to choose their shop to make the website for.

This website is where customers can check on what the bakery shop sell (custom birthday cakes, cupcakes, crepes, etc.), and get to know a bit about them and the location. On the cupcake page, all the different cupcakes are listed and you can hover on any specific one to see the details of it. The shop is famous for their custom cakes for birthdays or special occasions so it is important to put images to showcase that, and provide contact information so that people can make a call and order.

I tried to make the website clear and simple, as that’s all a local bakery brand needed. Please note that this is not Sini’s actual website.
