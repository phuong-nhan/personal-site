ONTARIO WILDLIFE RESCUE

Website Redesigning

Self-directed Project

Redesigning a website of an organization that I feel interested about. The selected one is the Ontario Wildlife Rescue Organization. The website should have functions that a typical nonprofit organization about animals rescuing have. The requirements are pulling the content from the original site only, the structure and approach should be created by myself, bringing a new perspective that is unique.

It is about redesigning a website that with a proper layout, more clearer than the original version. I considered bringing jungle, wildlife vibes into the design by using color such as cream brown, brown, dark green, orange. Photos of animals should be edited carefully, all images are cohesive and high resolution.
