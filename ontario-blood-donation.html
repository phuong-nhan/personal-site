ONTARIO BLOOD DONATION

Social Media Campaign

Self-directed Project

The orginal idea of the project is designing a social good campaign that requires user interactions. I created a campaign for Canadian Blood Services Center. The illustration was created by me with an idea of sending blood between people - a heart to a heart. Before starting the design, many research have been done to find the meaning of my chosen illustration.

The campaign also includes narrative illustrations for using on sign banner, social media accounts and a website.
