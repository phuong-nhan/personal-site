WORLD DISH OF THE DAY

UI/UX Design

Self-directed Project

The idea is to create and design an Application connecting people in both SPACE and TIME aspects. By using this application, the users from all around the world can daily vote for the dish that they eat everyday, eventually suggesting that for other people who might not know what should they eat.

The app lets users know not only one dish voted by everyone in the world but also by each continents. You can search and vote for your dish once a day. Every dish has its own description page. If you want to explore dishes, they are also listed under different countries and differents categories. This app will help you share and know different cuisines to other people.
